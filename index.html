FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Is this safe to use?

Yes! The app is completely open source and doesn't collect any data. It only stores your login session cookie, just like a web browser.

Do I need to log in every time?

No! After your first login, the app remembers your session. You'll stay logged in until you manually log out.

Why does Windows show a security warning?

The app isn't code-signed (costs $300+/year). This is normal for free open-source software. Click "More info" → "Run anyway" to install.

Will my messages be private?

Absolutely! This app is just a window to messenger.com. Your messages go directly to Facebook's servers. The app doesn't read or store them.

Can I use multiple accounts?

Not currently. You'll need to log out and log back in to switch accounts. Multi-account support may come in a future update!

How do I uninstall?

Go to Windows Settings → Apps → Find "Messenger Desktop (Unofficial)" → Click Uninstall. All app data will be removed.
